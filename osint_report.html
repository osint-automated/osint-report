The Russian Defense Ministry claimed to have shot down 4,300 Ukrainian drones in December, setting a record. The Ukrainian Foreign Ministry hinted that the strike on Kherson was a premeditated attack targeting civilians, while the Russian government accused Ukraine of committing war crimes. Elsewhere, U.S. intelligence found no evidence that Ukraine targeted Putin's residence, countering claims from the Kremlin regarding an attempted drone strike. Additionally, Lithuania protested to Russia over alleged war crimes committed by its forces in Ukraine, reinforcing ongoing tensions in the region.
